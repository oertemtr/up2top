Power Transformers, Cables & Electrical Equipment

Step-up transformers, step-down transformers, three-phase power transformers, distribution transformers (pole-mounted, pad-mounted), autotransformers, instrument transformers (CT, PT), isolation transformers, dry-type transformers, oil-immersed transformers, cast resin transformers. Circuit breakers, switchgear, surge arresters, relay protection panels, gas-insulated switchgear (GIS).

Auto Parts & Vehicle Components

OEM auto body parts: bumpers, fenders, hoods, doors, side mirrors, windshields, headlights, grilles. Drivetrain: drive axles, differentials, CV joints, propeller shafts, clutch assemblies, transmission parts. Suspension: shock absorbers, struts, control arms, ball joints, tie rods, steering racks, stabilizer bars. Brakes: brake pads, discs, drums, calipers, ABS sensors. Engine: pistons, cylinder heads, turbochargers, fuel injectors, timing belts. Wheels: alloy wheels, steel wheels, wheel bearings, TPMS sensors.

Hazelnuts, Pistachios, Dried Fruits & Biscuits

Turkish hazelnuts: raw (in-shell, shelled), roasted, blanched, hazelnut flour, hazelnut paste, hazelnut oil, chopped/diced/sliced, organic hazelnuts. Antep pistachios: in-shell, shelled, roasted, raw kernels, pistachio paste, green peeled. Dried fruits: dried figs (Aydin, Smyrna), dried apricots (Malatya), sultana raisins, sun-dried tomatoes, dried mulberries. Biscuits: petit beurre, digestive, cream-filled wafers, chocolate-coated biscuits, Turkish delight (lokum), halva, baklava, sesame bars.

Fish & Seafood

Farmed fish: European sea bass (levrek), gilthead sea bream (çipura), rainbow trout, salmon trout, Atlantic salmon, meagre. Wild catch: anchovy (hamsi), bonito (palamut), bluefish (lüfer), red mullet (barbunya), horse mackerel (istavrit), turbot (kalkan). Processed: frozen fillets, smoked fish, fish fingers, breaded fish, canned tuna, fish meal, fish oil, marinated anchovies. EU approved, HACCP, BRC, IFS, ASC, MSC, Halal, FDA registered.

Refined Petroleum & Petrochemicals

Refined products: gasoline (RON 92/95/98), diesel (EN 590), jet fuel (Jet A-1), fuel oil, LPG, naphtha, bitumen, base oils, lubricants. Aromatics: benzene, toluene, xylene (ortho/meta/para), mixed xylenes, styrene monomer, cumene, cyclohexane. Petrochemicals: polyethylene (HDPE, LDPE, LLDPE), polypropylene, PVC resin, PET resin, ethylene glycol, methanol. Specialty: white spirit, petroleum jelly, paraffin wax, carbon black, sulfur.

Copper Products & Alloys

Copper cathodes (LME Grade A), electrolytic copper, copper billets, copper ingots. Wire: copper wire rod (8mm), drawing quality wire, enameled copper wire, tinned copper wire, copper bus bars, copper strips/foils. Tubes: ACR copper tubes, plumbing copper pipes, medical gas tubes, copper fittings (elbows, tees, couplings), capillary tubes. Alloys: brass rods, brass sheets, brass valves, bronze products, copper-nickel alloys, beryllium copper.

Mining & Metals

Zinc: ores and concentrates, zinc metal (SHG 99.995%), zinc alloys, zinc oxide, zinc dust, hot-dip galvanizing. Ferro-chromium: high carbon (HC FeCr), low carbon (LC FeCr), charge chrome, chrome ore, stainless steel raw materials. Cobalt: cobalt carbonates, cobalt hydroxide, cobalt sulfate, battery-grade cobalt, cobalt metal powder. Other: ferro-manganese, ferro-silicon, silicon metal, feldspar, boron products (borax, boric acid), marble, natural stone, pumice.
