Why Türkiye for Services?

Türkiye has become a global hub for medical and education tourism through a combination of cost advantage, world-class infrastructure, English-speaking professionals, and a strategic location bridging Europe, the Middle East, and Central Asia.

🏥

World-Class Infrastructure

Over 70 JCI-accredited hospitals across major cities. Modern surgical theatres, robotic-assisted procedures, and Level III NICU and ICU facilities.

💰

Cost Advantage

Treatment and tuition costs run 50–70% lower than UK, Germany, or the US — with no compromise on quality, technology, or surgeon credentials.

👨‍⚕️

Trained Professionals

Surgeons and academics frequently trained or fellowship-certified in the US, UK, or EU. Multilingual support staff fluent in English, Arabic, Russian, and French.

🛫

Easy Travel

Visa-free access for 90+ countries. Daily direct flights from London, Frankfurt, Dubai, Moscow, Lagos, Cairo, Jeddah and Almaty. Istanbul Airport handles 90M+ passengers annually.

📋

Government Backing

Health Tourism is a state-supported sector under the Ministry of Health (USHAŞ). Education sector overseen by YÖK ensures recognised degrees in 100+ countries.

🤝

How It Works

A clear four-step process from your first inquiry to a successful outcome.

01

You Send an Inquiry

Tell us the service you need, your medical/academic background, target dates, and budget range. Use our form, WhatsApp, email, or AI assistant — whichever suits you.

02

We Match & Verify

Our team identifies 2–3 verified Turkish partners that best fit your case. We share their credentials, accreditations, and indicative packages — anonymously at first if you prefer.

03

You Choose, We Coordinate

You confirm the partner. We then handle scheduling, contracts, document preparation, visa support, travel and hotel coordination — all in one transparent commercial agreement.

04

Delivery & Follow-Up

During your stay you have a dedicated ExportTurk coordinator. After treatment or enrolment, we maintain follow-up communication and assist with any post-service needs.
